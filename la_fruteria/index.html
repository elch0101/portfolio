La Frutería

lafruteria.app

07/2020 - Present

La Frutería, founded in May 2020 in Mexico City, is a chain of grocery stores specializing in the quality & freshness of fruits and vegetables.

The objective: To offer the best quality & freshness of products at the same price as a supermarket to ensure customers are buying the best.

La Frutería has its app, where customers can order, pay and receive their favorite products from the commodity of their home, no matter the day of the week.
It offers same-day delivery, cutting spoiling times and ensuring the freshness and quality of the products.
