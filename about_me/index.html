Hey there, my name is Elias Cohen...

And for the past 2+ years, I've been expanding my professional career in product management. Thus far, I've learned and loved every part of this journey.

My mission as a product manager is to create a safe, transparent, communicative, closely-knit, and productive team so that we can construct outstanding products for our customers. By spending our time together and getting to know each other, we become more efficient; when to dig deep and when to pull back, who to turn to when we meet an obstacle and how to give constructive feedback positively.

My objective in the last two years has been to narrow the gap between design, engineering, and business to operate as a unit. Even though each team member is responsible for a different area, our combined skills enable us to become more efficient through trust and communication.

The secret is to maintain the focus of engineer's, designers, product managers, and stakeholder's investment in precisely who the customers are, the issues we're fixing, or the opportunity we're creating. Ultimately, we are working towards the same goal: improving someone's life by creating a product that meets their specific needs.
