07/2020 - October 2022

In the bustling realm of e-commerce and deliveries, there's a portal that stands out, a beacon named Sender. At its core, Sender isn't just a platform, but a promise. A promise that every parcel selected for delivery embarks on an efficient journey, with each step of its voyage transparent and traceable, ensuring it reaches its destination punctually.

What makes Sender unique? Its position as an intermediary. This vantage point allows Sender to cast its vision beyond just logistics. Its horizon encompasses the art of marketing, the craft of UI & UX design, and the essence of simplifying interactions for its vast user base.

By streamlining the complexities of shipment, Sender isn't merely facilitating deliveries. It's curating experiences. Whether a user is sending a gift to a loved one or a business shipping its products, Sender ensures that every parcel's tale is one of reliability and satisfaction.
